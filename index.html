The Trucking Simulator Game is designed to replicate a trucker's daily life, based on delivering loads across multiple cities and calculating operating costs of fuel and time. Over the next four days, your mission is to make as much money you can while your traveling through out cities ,in order for you to win you must return to your home city (city one ) by the 4th day and make over $4,000 . good luck !
